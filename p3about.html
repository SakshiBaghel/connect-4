How To Play

Connect 4 is a two-player connection game in which the players take turns dropping colored discs from the top into a vertically suspended grid. The objective is to connect four of one's own discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent. Here are the steps to play Connect 4:

  1. Set Up the Game: The game consists of a 6x7 grid (6 rows and 7 columns). Each player chooses a color (usually red or yellow) and takes turns dropping one of their colored discs into any of the seven columns from the top.
  2. Decide Who Goes First: Typically, players toss a coin or use some other random method to decide who goes first.
  3. Take Turns Dropping Discs: The players take turns dropping one of their discs into any of the open columns. The disc will fall down to the lowest available empty space within the selected column.
  4. Goal: Connect Four Discs: The goal is to be the first to connect four of your own discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The connection can occur in any direction—up and down, across, or diagonally.
  5. Block Opponent's Moves: As you play, try to block your opponent from making a winning connection of four discs. Also, work on creating your own opportunities to connect four discs
  6. Winning: The game ends as soon as one of the players successfully connects four of their discs in a row. The connection can be in any direction—horizontal, vertical, or diagonal
  7. Draw: If the entire grid is filled, and no player has four discs in a row, the game is a draw
  8. Start a New Game: After the game is finished, you can start a new one with the same or different players.
